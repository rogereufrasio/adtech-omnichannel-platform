# Business Context

## Company Overview

ShopSphere is a fictional omnichannel retailer operating:

- E-commerce
- Mobile Application
- Marketplace
- Loyalty Program
- Physical Stores

The company manages millions of customer interactions every month across digital and physical channels.

---

## Current Challenges

The current AdTech ecosystem has evolved organically over time and presents significant architectural challenges:

- Fragmented customer data across platforms
- Inconsistent event tracking standards
- Limited audience activation capabilities
- Delayed campaign attribution
- Heavy dependence on batch integrations
- Lack of end-to-end observability

---

## Business Drivers

The executive board has defined the following strategic priorities:

1. Improve marketing efficiency
2. Increase customer lifetime value
3. Enable personalized customer experiences
4. Reduce operational complexity
5. Improve compliance with LGPD

---

## Target Outcomes

The transformation initiative aims to achieve:

- Unified Customer Profile (Customer 360)
- Real-Time Audience Segmentation
- Omnichannel Activation
- Event-Driven Architecture
- Improved Attribution Models
- Privacy by Design
